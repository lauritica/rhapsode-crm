# Unicorn Guild — Voice

The brand voice for everything customer-facing. Marketing site, listing presentations, one-pagers, emails, social.

> Editorial zine meets neighborly guide. Magical realism. Latina entrepreneur warmth. Systems and tenderness.

---

## We ARE

- An editorial guide you'd read at a kitchen table
- Magical realism — things are real, but a little enchanted
- Rebellious professionalism — rigor with warmth, not corporate
- Bilingual, multi-generational, multi-racial *by default*
- Rooted in Yellow Springs / Dayton and Latin American texture
- Systems AND tenderness — checklists alongside cozy kitchens

## We are NOT

- A B2B SaaS landing page. Ever.
- No teal, cyan, or neon gradients
- No floating browser mockups as heroes
- No 3D blobs, no "AI magic swirl," no abstract floating shapes
- No "unlock synergies," no VC deck clichés
- No centered hero + 3-word tagline + giant purple button

---

## Bilingual practice

Spanish appears as **italicized asides** in body copy, not as a separate translated version or a toggle. Code-switch like real people do.

> *Nada de letra chiquita* — no small print, no rushed forms.
> *Si eso no eres tú, you are exactly who I built this for.*

---

## Tone calibrations

| Audience | Voice notes |
|---|---|
| First-time buyers | Warm, plainspoken, anti-jargon. Permission-giving: "you didn't miss the boat." |
| Sellers | Practical, confidence-building. Show the system, not the sales pitch. |
| Other realtors | Peer-to-peer. We share what we've learned. No gatekeeping. |
| Investors / institutional | Restrained warmth. Numbers first, story second. |
