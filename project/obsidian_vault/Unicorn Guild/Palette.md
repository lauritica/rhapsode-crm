# Unicorn Guild — Palette

Warm off-whites do the heavy lifting. Clay and moss ground the page. Sky gives breath. Mural-paint accents are used like a highlighter — small hits, never fills.

---

## Earth · grounding (the paper and the soil)
| Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Cream | `#F6F0E6` | Page background, paper |
| Bone | `#FBF6EC` | Hero backgrounds |
| Cream · deep | `#EFE7D7` | Card bg, subtle sections |
| Clay | `#C95E3E` | Primary accent, action buttons |
| Clay · deep | `#9C3F24` | Hover/pressed, headline pop |
| Clay · pale | `#F2D8CC` | Tints, badges |

## Sky & moss (Yellow Springs mornings, Costa Rica hillsides)
| Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Moss | `#4B6B3E` | Forest green |
| Moss · deep | `#2E4529` | Strong moss accents |
| Moss · pale | `#D5DCC3` | Background tints |
| Sky | `#7FA6B8` | Cool counterpoint |
| Sky · deep | `#4F7B92` | Strong sky accents |
| Sky · pale | `#D8E3E7` | Background tints |

## Ink · espresso (warm near-black)
| Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Espresso | `#1F1713` | Body text |
| Espresso · 2 | `#4A3C34` | Secondary ink |
| Espresso · 3 | `#7B6A5E` | Captions, muted |
| Espresso · 4 | `#B0A091` | Dividers, placeholders |

## Mural paint (use sparingly — like highlighter on a page)
| Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Highlighter | `#F4C95D` | Tape, marginalia |
| Marigold | `#E38B2E` | Call-outs |
| Plum | `#7A3E5E` | Italic emphasis |
| Rose | `#D26B6B` | Warm dusty accent |

---

## Banned (do not use, ever)
- Teal, cyan, electric blue
- Neon / fluorescent anything
- Pure cold black `#000000` (use Espresso instead)
- Pure cold white `#FFFFFF` (use Bone or Cream)
- Gradients on buttons or cards
